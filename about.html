About

Where this journey began, and why.

✦ The Origin

Born from a Tradition of Love

Self Realization Official was founded through the spiritual guidance, blessings, and teachings of Makhdoom Mahmood Mastwaar Qalandar — a Sufi master, teacher, poet, and the founder of Muhabbat Mission International.

Rooted in the philosophy of Muhabbat — Divine Love — this platform exists as a small digital extension of a much greater mission: to help people reconnect with the heart, recognize the signs of Allah within creation, and awaken to a deeper understanding of themselves.

Self Realization is a branch of Muhabbat Mission International and is managed by Muhammad Ali Kamdar. Its purpose is not merely to inform, entertain, or preach, but to invite reflection.

✦ The Vision

Unity Beneath All Things

The reflections shared here draw from Islamic spirituality, Sufi philosophy, ancient wisdom traditions, psychology, art, philosophy, literature, symbolism, and the lived human experience itself.

We believe truth leaves traces everywhere. In silence. In suffering. In beauty. In love.
In loss. In the patterns of nature and the movements of the soul.

At times, ideas explored by thinkers and mystics such as Ibn Arabi, Mawlana Rumi, Imam Ghazali, Allama Iqbal, and others may appear within the reflections — not as doctrines to impose,
but as lenses through which we contemplate existence, consciousness, the self, and our relationship with Allah.

“Wheresoever ye turn, there is the Face of Allah.”

— Qur'an 2:115

Across cultures, civilizations, and philosophies, we search for echoes of the same eternal truths — and relate them back to Islam, not as a limitation, but as illumination.

✦ The Approach

Reflection Over Noise

This is not a space for sectarian arguments, ideological warfare, or empty outrage.

The modern world constantly demands reaction. Self Realization chooses contemplation instead.

The articles are written slowly and intentionally — sometimes inspired by classical Sufi literature, sometimes by ordinary moments: a passing conversation, a sleepless night, a crowded street, or the strange silence that arrives after the world becomes too loud.

“There is nothing in existence but Him; everything else is but His shadow.”

— Ibn Arabi
✦ The Mission

A Reminder for the Seeking Heart

Through articles, videos, conversations, and reflection, the mission remains simple: to inspire sincerity, self-awareness, remembrance of Allah, and love for humanity.

In a distracted age, remembrance becomes rebellion. Stillness becomes medicine. And love becomes a path back home.
